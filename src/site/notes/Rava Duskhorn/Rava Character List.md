# Religious Connections
## Cyric
The God of Lies

Intent on gaining power, [[Rava Duskhorn/Rava Character List#Cyric\|Cyric]] developed the idea of founding a community who all lived in a lie. He found an elf, [[Rava Duskhorn/Rava Character List#The Blessed Intavar\|Intavar]] and offered him power in exchange for his aid. Together they developed a [[Rava Duskhorn/Rava The Church of Otar#Dogma\|dogma]], holy texts, [[Rava Duskhorn/Rava The Church of Otar#Rites\|rites]], [[Rava Duskhorn/Rava The Church of Otar#Oxtun\|a language]], [[Rava Duskhorn/Rava The Church of Otar#Holidays\|holidays]], etc. While the project grew from one city ([[Rava Duskhorn/Rava The Church of Otar#Otarere\|Otarere]]) to three, [[Rava Duskhorn/Rava Character List#Cyric\|Cyric]]'s influence continues to grow. 

Beyond this, not much is known about [[Rava Duskhorn/Rava Character List#Cyric\|Cyric]] and his motivations are unknown.
