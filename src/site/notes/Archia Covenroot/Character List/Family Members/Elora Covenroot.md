# Elora Covenroot
- The 2nd child of [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Family Members/Kali Covenroot\|Kali]] and [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Family Members/Edmar Covenroot\|Edmar]] & Archia's sister
- Was incredibly close with Archia throughout their childhood
- Got an apprenticeship as a seamstress to help her family shortly after Archia left
- Elora went on to be the head of a team of seamstresses for a noble family
	- This job allows her to provide for her entire family
- She misses Archia, but is still angry with her for leaving
