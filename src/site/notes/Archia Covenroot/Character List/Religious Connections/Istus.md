# Istus
- Goddess of the Weave of Fate
- She largely unknown to most people
- Her preferred appearance is that of a woman in her mid 20's with dark skin and opalescent hair
- She likes tea
- She is one of the few gods who has a positive relationship with [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Religious Connections/The Raven Queen\|The Raven Queen]]
	- The Raven Queen forged Reaver for Archia as a favor for Istus
- Istus cannot bend the will of fate, and will not directly interfere with the outcome of events, she can however change how they happen
	-   This is why she always has her two harbingers; The Harbinger of Chaos and [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Religious Connections/The Harbinger of Order\|The Harbinger of Order]]
	-   If one of her harbingers dies, a new one is chosen
