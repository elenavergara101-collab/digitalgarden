# Carodre Varak
- High moon elf, slender, long hair that's often tied up
- He has severe acid burns from a dragon running across his arms, neck, and face
- Long standing member of the Burden of Courage
- Bosun of [[Archia Covenroot/Misc Details/Neptune's Executioner\|Neptune's Executioner]]
- Archia's mentor
	- They have a very close bond, and he acts almost like an older brother or close uncle would; he is very protective of her
- When Archia left the Budren of Courage he gifted her a silver necklace
