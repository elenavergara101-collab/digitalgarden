# Abbreviated Backstory 
- Archia's mother, [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Family Members/Kali Covenroot\|Kali Druun]], was born an elven noble, but broke off her arranged marriage to marry [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Family Members/Edmar Covenroot\|Edmar Covenroot]], a poor human man.
- Archia was the oldest of her 7 siblings: [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Family Members/Elora Covenroot\|Elora]], [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Family Members/Aym Covenroot\|Aym]] & [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Family Members/Brayer Covenroot\|Brayer]], [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Family Members/Talen Covenroot\|Talen]], and [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Family Members/Pirev Covenroot\|Pirev]], [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Family Members/Voma Covenroot\|Voma]] & [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Family Members/Karai Covenroot\|Karai]].
- Archia's mother became increasingly more frail to the point where she could no longer work.
- Her family often went hungry, and Archia began running small errands for mercenary groups to help provide.
- She made a deal with one of these groups, and when she turned 14 she left to become a member of the Burden of Courage.
- She and her mentor [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/The Burden of Courage/Carodre Varak\|Carodre Varak]] spent most of their time sailing, but he would also bring her along on other adventures so she had plenty of experience.
- During one of these adventures, Archia found a map and became immediately enthralled with it.
- She parted ways with her mentor, and after a very long search, found [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Religious Connections/Istus\|Istus']] temple.
- There she formed her [[Archia Covenroot/Pact/Pact Agreement\|pact]] and was gifted her sword, [[Archia Covenroot/Misc Details/Reaver\|Reaver]].
