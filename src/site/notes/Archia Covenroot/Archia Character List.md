# Family Members

## Edmar Covenroot
- Archia's Father
- Married and fathered a total of 8 children with [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Kali Covenroot\|Kali]]
- Had a difficult time accepting that he wasn't able to provide for all of his children
- Was frequently absent due to his work
## Kali Covenroot
- Formally Kali Druun (maiden name)
- Archia's Mother
- She broke of her engagement to [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Taegin Canthaalor\|Taegin Canthaalor]] for [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Edmar Covenroot\|Edmar Covenroot]]
- She had a total of 8 children over her lifetime
## Elora Covenroot
- The 2nd child of [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Kali Covenroot\|Kali]] and [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Edmar Covenroot\|Edmar]] & Archia's sister
- Was incredibly close with Archia throughout their childhood
- Got an apprenticeship as a seamstress to help her family shortly after Archia left
- Elora went on to be the head of a team of seamstresses for a noble family
	- This job allows her to provide for her entire family
- She misses Archia, but is still angry with her for leaving
## Aym Covenroot
- The 3rd child of [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Kali Covenroot\|Kali]] and [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Edmar Covenroot\|Edmar]] & Archia's sister
- Twin of [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Brayer Covenroot\|Brayer]] (mirrored twins)
- Thanks to Elora, she was able to go to a boarding wizarding school, where she is now
## Brayer Covenroot
- The 4th child of [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Kali Covenroot\|Kali]] and [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Edmar Covenroot\|Edmar]] & Archia's brother

## Talen Covenroot

# Religious Connections
## Istus
- Goddess of the Weave of Fate
- She largely unknown to most people
- Her preferred appearance is that of a woman in her mid 20's with dark skin and opalescent hair
- She likes tea
- She is one of the few gods who has a positive relationship with [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#The Raven Queen\|The Raven Queen]]
	- The Raven Queen forged Reaver for Archia as a favor for Istus
- Istus cannot bend the will of fate, and will not directly interfere with the outcome of events, she can however change how they happen
	-   This is why she always has her two harbingers; The Harbinger of Chaos and [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#The Harbinger of Order\|The Harbinger of Order]]
	-   If one of her harbingers dies, a new one is chosen

## The Harbinger of Order
- The identity of this harbinger is unknown to Archia
- All she knows is that they will act to bestow order; as a counterpart to her chaos

## The Raven Queen
- The Goddess of Death
- She forges all hexblade weapons
- In her [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Details#Pact Agreement\|pact agreement]], Archia agreed to remain reverent to the Raven Queen and Her Domain
