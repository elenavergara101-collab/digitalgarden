# Potential Plot Points
## Family
- Archia runs in to any family member, especially [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Family Members/Elora Covenroot\|Elora]]
- Archia is confronted by an NPC or PC about her abandoning her family

## Istus
- [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Religious Connections/Istus\|Istus]] gives an order that Archia is extremely hesitant to [[Archia Covenroot/Pact/Pact Agreement#^cc5c05\|carry out]]
- If a party member dies and is revivified: [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Religious Connections/The Raven Queen\|The Raven Queen]] demands the debt be settled
- Conflict with [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Religious Connections/The Harbinger of Order\|The Harbinger of Order]]

## Mercenaries
- Archia reunites with her [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/The Burden of Courage/Carodre Varak\|mentor]], [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/The Burden of Courage/Kyros Tolbrov\|captain]], or [[Archia Covenroot/Misc Details/Neptune's Executioner\|ship]]
- Archia discovers [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Family Members/Brayer Covenroot\|Brayer]] joined the Burden of Courage
- Archia is called back to aid her group during a dispute with another mercenary group
