# Pact Agreement

1.  I swear myself eternally to the Goddess [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Religious Connections/Istus\|Istus]], the weaver of fate.
2.  I, as a harbinger of fate, will sow chaos by disrupting the threads of fate and changing predetermined events. My anonymous [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Religious Connections/The Harbinger of Order\|counterpart]] will counteract my actions, and will bestow order to the weave of fate.
4.  I will allow my counterpart to remain anonymous and will not attempt to find them.
5.  My mission is my own and will not be shared to any mortal or divine being unless explicitly allowed by my patron. 
6.  I will follow all commands given to me by my patron.
{ #cc5c05}

7.  Any request of mine made to Istus may be denied, but will always be considered. 
8.  If I wish an audience with Istus, she will grant the audience with me in, at most, three days time.
9.  My blade, [[Archia Covenroot/Misc Details/Reaver\|Reaver]], was made for Istus as a gift from [[Archia Covenroot/Character List/Religious Connections/The Raven Queen\|The Raven Queen]]. While I do not need to pray or present offerings, I must maintain a reverence of her and her domain. If I am ever to bring a soul out of the Raven Queen’s domain, I must give her a new one to replace what I have taken. 
10.  My name and title now is: Archia Covenroot, devotee of Istus, [[Archia Covenroot/Misc Details/Other Details#^534a7a\|heir]] of the Druun family, mercenary of the Burden of Courage, wielder of the [[Archia Covenroot/Misc Details/Reaver\|Reaving Blade]], and the Acting Harbinger of Chaos.
