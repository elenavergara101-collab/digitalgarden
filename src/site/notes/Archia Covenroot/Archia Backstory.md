- She and her mentor [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Carodre Varak\|Carodre Varak]] spent most of their time sailing, but he would also bring her along on other adventures so she had plenty of experience.

- She parted ways with her mentor, and after a very long search, found [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Istus\|Istus']] temple.
- There she formed her [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Details#Pact Agreement\|pact]] and was gifted her sword, [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Details#Reaver\|Reaver]].

# Full Backstory
Archia's mother, [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Kali Covenroot\|Kali Druun]], was born a high elf into a noble house, but she had been disowned the second she broke off her arranged marriage with [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Taegin Canthaalor\|Taegin Canthaalor]]. She instead married a human named [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Edmar Covenroot\|Edmar Covenroot]], who had only a tiny one room house in an unkempt corner of Neverwinter to his name. The two made a life for themselves, and while they weren’t well off, they were content. Soon after they were married, Archia was born. 
___
Only four months after, Kali found herself pregnant again. Months later, Archia got the chance to meet her little sister, [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Elora Covenroot\|Elora]]. Although Archia was barely a year older than Elora,  Archia took the role of older sister very seriously. They were inseparable. On Archia’s 6th birthday she was told that she was going to be a big sister again.  Archia and Elora were thrilled. To both of their surprise, rather than one new sibling they gained two. [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Aym Covenroot\|Aym]] and [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Brayer Covenroot\|Brayer]], mirrored twins, were born just a few minutes apart from each other.
{ #a1246b}


Shortly after the twin’s birth Archia began noticing the tension within her family. Her mother used to work as a seamstress, but she had to stop so she could care for the twins. And despite the fact that Archia’s father worked dawn till dusk in the fields just outside of the city, they were barely making enough to get by.
{ #3ba191}


Archia, scared for her family, decided that she would try to help provide for her family as best she could. Despite her parents’ very strong disapproval, the two girls ran around doing favors for whatever coin they could get. Over the next few years Archia began running errands for the mercenary companies more frequently, slowly becoming a welcome sight in the inns and taverns that often housed these adventurers. She became friends with a few, and despite the fact many of them didn’t have much for themselves, they did their best to provide Archia with at least enough money for a meal.
{ #339e69}


 While Archia was working amongst the mercenaries, Elora was taken in as an apprentice under a seamstress of one of the noble houses thanks to her mother’s reputation. money, but the training provided her with the guarantee of a better future. 
{ #1c8dfc}


Kali gave birth twice more during Archia’s childhood.
  
The next child was born 3 years after the twins. Their fourth sibling was a very frail boy they named [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Talen Covenroot\|Talen]]. Born both blind and deaf, Talen required constant attention and care, but despite their efforts, he died barely five months later. The grief weighed very heavily on the family for the next few years. 

After the grief of Talen's death faded, Archia’s mother got pregnant with what would be her last child. She became incredibly frail during her pregnancy, and Archia began needing to balance her work and caring for her mother. They were barely getting by, and often Archia and her father would go hungry to ensure everyone else could eat.

Archia finally decided to act, and she made an agreement with the Burden of Courage, a mercenary group she had been doing jobs growing up. Her familiarity with them made the group her first choice, a mix of practicality and loyalty. On her 14th birthday, Archia was to report to a senior member of the group and begin her training. While she wouldn’t receive any wage until she was much older, Archia would have her basic needs completely met, and her parents would have one less mouth to feed. Scared her family wouldn’t allow her to leave, she made the decision to leave without warning or goodbyes.

Only 5 days before Archia was supposed to leave, Her mother went into labor. This time giving birth to triplets, 2 boys, [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Pirev Covenroot\|Pirev]] and [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Voma Covenroot\|Voma]], and a girl they named [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Karai Covenroot\|Karai]]. All three were frail and tiny, but healthy. For the next 5 days Archia choked down the guilt and hesitation building in her throat. She tried her best to celebrate alongside her family. Late that spring, Archia left just a little before dawn, taking with her only her clothing, a dagger, and the only memento she would allow herself, one of Elora's hair ribbons. 
___
At one of the taverns she frequented, Archia finally met her long awaited mentor for the first time. Waiting for her was a High Moon Elf, pale skinned, tall and slender, with white hair and pale blue eyes.  His sleeves were rolled up to reveal several acid burn scars running along his arms. Parts of his neck and face were burned in the same way. His name was [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Carodre Varak\|Carodre Varak]], and he worked as the bosun for a vessel known as [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Details#Neptune's Executioner\|Neptune's Executioner]]. Archia quickly took her place amongst the crew, conditioning and training every second she could between her work caring for the vessel. Carodre would push Archia to her limits again and again, and for four years this was her home. Although he would occasionally take Archia to do other business on land, so she would gain experience in other forms of work. 

On one of these excursions, Archia found a rather old map. And while Carodre dismissed the collections of scrawlings, Archia immediately and inexplicably became fixated on the idea of whatever was at the end of this path. Despite her better judgment, she decided to track down whatever was waiting for her, and she, for the first time, set off without her mentor. 
___ 
Archia spent close to two years on her journey, piecing together snippets of maps and other scrawlings that slowly led her closer to whatever she was looking for. Following the third map that she had pieced together, Archia found a small and long forgotten temple. She walked over the collapsed roof to the statue that stood in the center. While the rest of the building had largely decayed, the statue of a sitting woman remained relatively intact. Archia dug through the rubble in an attempt to find a name plate. She instead found a sword, A long elegant blade that reeked of power. She pulled the sword from the pile it had been buried under, and as she looked up, blade in her hand, the temple had transformed itself around her. 

The building that once lay in ruin was now a beautiful chapel, bright light shining through the large ornate stained glass windows. A small table sat where just moments before the statue had been. This was the temple of a goddess, and for the first time in many years she invited someone into her home. Archia had been granted an audience. 

She appeared only a few years older than Archia, who was 19 at the time. She had iridescent hair and eyes, dark skin, and kind features. She set her knitting down when Archia arrived, the fabric alone was entrancing as the yarn was made of the sky and stars that filled it. The woman began pouring two cups of tea for them, making it exactly to Archia’s knowledge without needing to ask. Similarly, Archia knew her, as if the knowledge of each other had always been present. [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Istus\|Istus]], the goddess and weaver of fate, looked up at Archia and smiled.

“Welcome, Archia, my harbinger”

Over several separate rounds of tea, Istus and Archia talked. The bright light outside the windows turned orange, then pink, then purple as the time grew later. They indulged themselves in a number of sweets and other foods. The taste of sugar and chocolate and honey was largely foreign to Archia, who was very much enjoying it.

At the end of the night, Istus waved her hand to rearrange the furniture. The pews of the old chapel were replaced with a bed much more plush then Archia had slept in. Istus invited her to rest, and she obliged. 

When Archia awoke, she was laying on the floor of the overgrown ruins, the embers of a fire she did not remember making lay dying beside her. In her hand, she held the [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Details#Pact Agreement\|agreement]] that she and her goddess had decided on the night prior. On the floor, lay her new sword, [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Details#Reaver\|Reaver]], now sheathed in a leather scabbard for her. And as a final parting gift, Istus left her a small bag with just a couple more candies.
