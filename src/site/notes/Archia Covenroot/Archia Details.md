## Details about Istus
-  Istus is a largely forgotten goddess. Even in documents cataloging gods and their domains, she’s only occasionally listed. In academia, there is an ongoing debate on her existence. Istus finds it amusing.
-   As Archia’s faith grows (as she levels up) Istus’ temple in her realm grows in size.
-   [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#The Raven Queen\|The Raven Queen]]'s power is still infused in [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Details#Reaver\|Reaver]] which "overflows" into Archia's magic.
	-   Archia's magic is cyan but it darkens to black upon impact (for attack spells) or after a period of 1 hour (for utility spells).
	-   She has a "tattoo" around her left eye that looks like ink swirls in water; as her power grows (as she levels up), the mark grows more complex.
{ #7fb222}

		-   At 4th level the swirls appear on her left hand. At 7th they appear on her right shoulder. At 10th they appear on her left calf.
		-   Between these milestones, the marks will spread across her arm, leg, back, and face.
		-   Beginning at 14th level and ending at 15th, the marks begin to change colors, from the black associated with the Raven Queen to iridescent cyan, which is associated with both Archia and Istus. 
			- Alternatively this will happen after a Raven Queen related plot point.

## Details about Archia
-   She has a massive sweet tooth.
-   She has a really soft spot for kids, and will go out of her way to help them.
-   She often struggles with the guilt of leaving her family.
-   She doesn’t want to face her family, especially Elora, so she generally tries to avoid the Neverwinter area.
-   Archia was relatively calm when she made her deal with Istus; it was a pivotal moment for her, but she was anxious about what was to come.
-   Technically her title would be [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Details#^bcbc12\|"bastard heir"]], but Istus chose to leave that out.
-   She hates when other people pay for her due to her past struggles being a burden on her family financially.
-   She feels very out of place in lavish areas.

# Pact Agreement

1.  I swear myself eternally to the Goddess [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#Istus\|Istus]], the weaver of fate.
2.  I, as a harbinger of fate, will sow chaos by disrupting the threads of fate and changing predetermined events. My anonymous [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#The Harbinger of Order\|counterpart]] will counteract my actions, and will bestow order to the weave of fate.
3.  I will allow my counterpart to remain anonymous and will not attempt to find them.
4.  My mission is my own and will not be shared to any mortal or divine being unless explicitly allowed by my patron. 
5.  I will follow all commands given to me by my patron.
{ #cc5c05}

6.  Any request of mine made to Istus may be denied, but will always be considered. 
7.  If I wish an audience with Istus, she will grant the audience with me in, at most, three days time.
8.  My blade, [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Details#Reaver\|Reaver]], was made for Istus as a gift from [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Character List#The Raven Queen\|The Raven Queen]]. While I do not need to pray or present offerings, I must maintain a reverence of her and her domain. If I am ever to bring a soul out of the Raven Queen’s domain, I must give her a new one to replace what I have taken. 
9.  My name and title now is: Archia Covenroot, devotee of Istus, [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Details#^dcbcc6\|heir]] of the Druun family, mercenary of the Burden of Courage, wielder of the [[Archia Covenroot/Archia Details#Reaver\|Reaving Blade]], and the Acting Harbinger of Chaos.
{ #bcbc12}
